About



Pura selongsong or Pura Rolling & Cone is a division under Pura Group. Our Product Detail

ICW (Individual Cigarette Wrapping)

Individual Cigarette Wrapping (ICW) or Selongsong is a cone-shaped packaging made of aluminum foil paper. The shape and size of the product are adjusted to each cigarette type. ICW can maintain the quality of taste and aroma of cigarettes, and also add aesthetic value to be perceived as a premium and luxurious product.

ECC (Empty Cigarette Cones) ​

Empty Cigarette Cones (ECC) are made by rolling special cigarette paper material, Reconstituted/Homogenized Tobacco Leaf (RTL/HTL), or Natural Leaf into a cone that fits the shape and size of cigarettes or mini cigars. ECC can be produced with or without filter tips and cigarette shield. Product dimensions, material choices and additional infused-flavor are optional according to the customer’s request.

Tobacco Stopper/ Cigarette Shield​

Tobacco Stopper (TS) or Cigarette Shield (CS) is a cylindrical-shaped product made of hand-rolled special paper. The main function of this innovation is as a barrier or blockade in an classic hand-rolled cigarette materials (e.g., tobacco, herbs, weed, etc) to make smoking experience even smoother and more enjoyable.

Filter Tips​

Filter tips are similar to a Tobacco Stopper/Cigarette Shield product, which has a cylindrical-shape, but the raw materials used for manufacturing this product are mostly natural leaf. Filter tips could also be used as a barrier or blockade (i.e., similar to tobacco stopper) for cigarette materials and also inventing new unique and mysterious sensations of a smoking experience.

Natural Cigarette Wrap​

Natural Cigarette Wrap are a product in the form of cigarette paper sheets that are made of natural leaf (e.g., Sugar Palm leaf, Corn Husk and Banana Leaf) or also Natural Leaf Paper (e.g., Reconstituted Sugar Palm Leaf paper and Reconstituted Corn Husk paper). The Natural Cigarette Wrap could also be processed for Empty Cigarette Cones (ECC).
