WRAPPER CANDY​

We can supply wrappers for candy which generally consists of 2 types of packaging, an inner wrapper, and an outer wrapper. This paper-based packaging can laminated with aluminum foil, PE, LLDPE, wax, or with a special coating that is adjusted to the characteristics of the candy.

PAPER BAG

The paper bag is an alternative packaging to replace plastic bags. You can choose the materials for your customized paper bag such as grease-proof, kraft, paper-coated, or metalized paper. SOAP WRAPPER​​

Soap is a delicate product that is susceptible to moisture and water damage. So, to protect the soap from moisture and other contaminants, having a bar soap wrapper is a must! We provide you the paper-based packaging that laminates with PET to keep your soap well-protected, and not only that, but the customers will also be impressed with visual appeal since you can customize the designs, colors, and sizes to represent your brand.

FOOD WRAPPER​​

We can supply food packaging depending on your food products. For example, to keep compressed yeast fresh, you need to wrap the yeast with Paper Wax because compressed yeast requires high-barrier packaging. If the product contains fat like butter, you can choose paper laminated foil packaging with a special coating for oil grease and water repellent. Remember, if the packaging does not match the character of the food product, it reduces its quality and shelf life. So, let's make your food packaging with us that is suitable for your food product.
