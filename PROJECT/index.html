Our Product Detail

Cigarette
Pack

Cigarette Packs consist of Cigarette Packs itself or HLP, Inner Frame and Inner Wrapper. We customized to fot our Customer needs with vaious type of design and color, various type of material, various added value such as carving, hot stamping foil, deboss, emboss, UV tactile, UV drip off, etc, we also can supply in roll or sheet for Inner Frame and Inner Wrapper.

HTL

HTL is a tobacco-based sheet product, can be used as a wrapper or binder in filtered cigar/cigarillo. HTL properties can be adjusted and compatible to be used in your cigarette maker, with good paper strength which increase productivity.

Cigarette
Tipping Paper

We provide to up 5 colors printing with white, yellow, and black base paper. Laser perforated 20 cu to 2000 cu, with 1 line to 4 perforating. Many kinds of coating process such as sweetener, flavored, and lip release coating, also pearlescent ink combined with hot stamping foil for luxurious appearances. We also provided cigarette paper / RYO paper with some added value such as pearlescent ink, and sweetener in line.

Total
Security System

We are supplying Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) Film use for wrapping products packaging. We also supply OPP Heatsealable + tear tape in sheet or rools specially made for cigarette packaging. Tear tape, also known as tearstrip, or tear-off ribbon, is a narrow adhesive tape used to open packaging such as Cigarette packs, cosmetics, F&B packaging, etc. HSF / Foil Stamping generally determined as application of hologram foil onto solid surface, making it permanently adhere to the surface below leaving the design of the dye. We customized to fit our customers needs with various type of design and color, hologram, printed or custom logo and transparent.

Selongsong

With the trend toward hollow filter pipes made of ivory and wood (with high production cost) are rising, it's becoming more important than ever to find an alternative special material that is not only more economical but also more luxurious. Our special material is expected to be able to provide a more varied taste because it can be given various kinds of sweeteners, aromas, and "Lip Release' as anticipation of wetness in the suction part of SKT (hand-rolled) cigarettes. We focused on manufacturing cigarette support products such as selongsong or Individual Cigarette Shields (CS), Filter Tips, and Empty Cigarette Cones (ECC). We manufacture to ensure smokers get the best smoking experience and provide the benefits of improving the appearance of the product, maintaining the aroma and quality, and placing more emphasis on product exclusivity by using local natural resources and special paper as our raw material.

Smart
Packaging

In addition to protecting products and attracting more customers, packaging has great potential as a means of brand protection, promotion, data collection and increasing overall business value. Pura as a large printing business group presentase comprehensive solution for Smart Packaging. Pura offers Pura Smart Pack solutions:

  1. SmartPack Secure: A solution to protect products from counterfeiting such as Secure Design, Secure IT, Secure Material.
  2. SmartPack Engange: A Solution to improve the relationship between the manufacturing industry and customers such as Augmented Reality, Customer Loyalty Management and Customer Relationship Management.
  3. SmartPack Active: Actively proects and increases the life of product by adding special meterials or coatings.
  4. SmartPack Notify: Notify Customers about the feasibility of the product to consume.

Inventory Management System

Inventory Management System is a system that helps track a company's inventory and sales. It supports employees by providing accurate inventory numbers and keeping products organized. This helps a company manage its stock, fill orders and track teh overall productions or sales.
